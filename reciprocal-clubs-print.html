Visiting another member club

A letter, a phone call, a welcome.

Each of the clubs that follows has a reciprocal arrangement with The Thames Club. Most welcome members for dining, lodging, or short visits — terms vary by club, and many require an introduction letter from our office before you arrive. We are glad to make the call.

Reach out at least a few days before you travel. We will confirm the club is expecting you, send a letter on Thames Club letterhead if needed, and pass along anything useful — dress code, lunch hours, whether they take walk-ins.

Request an introduction letter

Call (959) 264‑2733 and ask for Jennifer at the Club office, or email thamesclub.nl@gmail.com. Please include the club name, the dates of your visit, and the names of anyone traveling with you.

If you are not sure whether a particular club is on the list, just ask — reciprocity arrangements change, and the office tracks updates as they happen.

About this directory

Last reviewed April 27, 2026.

This directory was reviewed in spring 2026. The clubs below have been removed from previous editions of the list — five after this review (closures or mergers since the prior printing), five flagged earlier and still pending office confirmation:

  • University Club of Phoenix — permanently closed June 2025.
  • The Quinnipiac Club, New Haven — merged with the Graduate Club in 2012 to form The Elm City Club; the Church Street building has been sold.
  • Sangamo Club, Springfield IL — closed June 2023 after 133 years.
  • The Beacon Club, Kalamazoo MI — closed 2019; the building is now a public restaurant.
  • University Club of Albany — closed 2022 after 121 years.
  • Corpus Christi Town Club, University Club of Orlando, University Club of Mizzou (MU) — appear to have closed; pending office confirmation.
  • Soundview Club and India House — verification signals were weak; pending office confirmation.

Reciprocity arrangements and operating status change. If you have firsthand information about any of these clubs — or about any club you believe should be on the list — please call the Club office at (959) 264‑2733 or write to thamesclub.nl@gmail.com.

Web addresses listed in this directory were verified during the April 2026 review. Reciprocity terms, hours of service, dress codes, and lodging availability vary by club and change without notice. Always call ahead, and request an introduction letter from the Thames Club office before your visit.
